Replica Media Group is dedicated to providing first-class video services to every client. We specialize in onsite event live-streaming. Our mission is to bring family and friends together for events that wouldn’t be possible otherwise. Every live-streamed event package includes a hard copy of the event to be relived once the event has ended. Health and safety is a huge part of our foundation and CDC COVID-19 guidelines will be followed at all times during an Replica event. Don’t let time, distance, or the coronavirus cause you to miss out on life’s most precious and valuable moments. Contact us today to see how we may be of service to you!
